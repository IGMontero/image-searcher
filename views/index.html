Image searcher

Usage:

Example Input:

  1. https://nachito333-image-searcher.glitch.me/api/imagesearch/funny%20search?offset=3
  2. https://nachito333-image-searcher.glitch.me/api/latest

1) Returns 10 objects that match your search ,containing url, snippet, thumbnail and context. You can add '?offset=n' to your query to paginate over the searches, where n is the page number.

2) Returns the 10 latest searches , including the date when each one was created.
